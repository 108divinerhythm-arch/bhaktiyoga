Who We Are

A Community Rooted in Wisdom

Bhakti Yoga Foundation is an ACNC-registered not-for-profit charity based in Queensland, Australia. We are a community of families, volunteers, and dedicated individuals united by a shared commitment to inner growth, character development, and conscious living.

Founded in October 2024 and active since January 2025, we run free weekly programs every Sunday in northern Brisbane — welcoming families from all cultural backgrounds with open arms. We are entirely self-funded through community contributions. Every dollar goes directly toward programs, venue hire, and free meals.

Legal & Registration Notice: Bhakti Yoga Foundation is the public operating name of Divine Rhythm Inc. — our registered legal entity. Both names refer to the same ACNC-registered charity (ABN 88 219 281 244). You can verify our charity status on the ACNC Charity Register. This is a common and fully compliant practice under Australian charity law.

Every Sunday · Northern Brisbane

Two Programs.
One Community.

Join us every Sunday at our free community programs. All are welcome — no prior experience or background needed. Just bring an open heart.

Morning Program · 9:30 AM – 11:00 AM
Spiritual Education Classes
  • Kids Class 1 (Ages up to 8) — Vedic culture, character education & spiritual values
  • Kids Class 2 (Ages 9–16) — Deeper age-appropriate exploration of wisdom & ethics
  • Adult Class — Bhagavad Gita discourse, practical wisdom & stress management
  • All three classes run simultaneously every Sunday
📍 Pendicup Community Centre
Samsonvale Road, Warner QLD 4500
Evening Program · 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM
Community Spiritual Festival
  • Kirtan — uplifting musical meditation & devotional chanting
  • Bhagavad Gita class with practical wisdom for daily life
  • Bhajans — devotional songs and group singing
  • Free vegan/vegetarian dinner — open to all, no booking needed
  • Regular senior & international guest speakers
📍 Pendicup Community Centre (Warner)
or Bray Hall Community Centre (Petrie)

Character & Ethics

🧘
