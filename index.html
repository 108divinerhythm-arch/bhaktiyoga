Who We Are

A Community Rooted in Wisdom

Bhakti Yoga Foundation (legally registered as Divine Rhythm Inc.) is an ACNC-registered not-for-profit charity based in Queensland, Australia. We are a community of families, volunteers, and dedicated individuals united by a shared commitment to inner growth, character development, and conscious living.

Founded in October 2024 and active since January 2025, our foundation has been running free weekly programs every Sunday in the northern Brisbane area. We are entirely self-funded through the generous contributions of our members — every dollar goes directly toward running programs, hiring community venues, and providing free meals.

Every Sunday

Two Programs.
One Community.

Join us every Sunday at our free community programs in northern Brisbane. All are welcome — no prior experience needed.

Morning Program · 9:30 AM – 11:00 AM
Spiritual Education Classes
  • Children (up to 8): Vedic culture, character education & spiritual values
  • Children (9–16): Deeper age-appropriate exploration of the same themes
  • Parents & Adults: Bhagavad Gita discourse — practical wisdom & stress management
  • All programs run simultaneously every Sunday
📍 Pendicup Community Centre
Samsonvale Road, Warner QLD 4500
Evening Program · 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM
Community Spiritual Festival
  • Kirtan — uplifting musical meditation
  • Bhagavad Gita class with practical wisdom for daily life
  • Bhajans — devotional songs and group chanting
  • Free vegan/vegetarian dinner — open to all
  • Regular senior & international guest speakers
